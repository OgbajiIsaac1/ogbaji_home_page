## Page Breakdown

### index.html - Landing Page
**Purpose**: Professional introduction and value proposition
**Sections**:
- Navigation header with logo and menu
- Hero section with generated image and typewriter headline
- Services overview with interactive cards
- Portfolio gallery with filtering
- Client testimonials carousel
- Call-to-action section
- Footer with contact information

### about.html - Company Information
**Purpose**: Build trust and showcase expertise
**Sections**:
- Navigation header
- Company story and mission
- Team member profiles with photos
- Company values and culture
- Timeline of achievements
- Footer

### services.html - Service Offerings
**Purpose**: Detailed service descriptions and pricing
**Sections**:
- Navigation header
- Service categories with filtering
- Detailed service cards with hover effects
- Pricing information
- Service process workflow
- Related case studies
- Footer

### contact.html - Contact Information
**Purpose**: Enable easy communication
**Sections**:
- Navigation header
- Contact form with validation
- Office locations and map
- Contact information display
- Business hours
- Social media links
- Footer
