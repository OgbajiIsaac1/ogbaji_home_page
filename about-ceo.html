Biography

The Visionary Behind the Mission

Isaac (Okpahe-Isom) Ogbaji-mMBA is a transformation-driven leader building at the intersection of education, technology, and innovation. He is passionate about creating intelligent systems that do more than function — systems that empower people, strengthen institutions, and unlock new possibilities for growth and impact.

"The future belongs to those who can learn, adapt, and build with clarity in a rapidly changing digital world."

As an EdTech consultant and AI educator, Isaac is committed to making artificial intelligence understandable, accessible, and actionable for everyday people, schools, and organizations. His work is rooted in a clear belief: the future belongs to those who can learn, adapt, and build with clarity in a rapidly changing digital world.

Moments That Define the Journey

Current Role

Director of Business Operations & Educational Services

He currently serves as Director of Business Operations and Educational Services at BrainCapital, where he contributes to strategic leadership, operational growth, and educational innovation. His experience spans curriculum design, entrepreneurship, technology adoption in schools, and the implementation of practical educational systems such as EMIS and Canvas LMS.

Isaac is known for bridging the gap between complex technology and human-centered solutions. He has a unique ability to translate emerging concepts into practical systems that help educators, learners, and institutions thrive.
